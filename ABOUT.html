ABOUT ME

Hey There!

My name is Gabby Brickner, and I am currently a sophomore here at the University of Georgia studying Marketing! I just recently got admitted to the Terry College of Business this October, so I am very excited to begin my major-specific courses in the spring of 2019! I am originally from North Carolina, but have moved, and traveled, around the country for the majority of my life. However, I moved to Georgia about four years ago to be closer to my family, and that is where I discovered my love for sculpting.

During my senior year of high school, I got the chance to learn the art of pottery and sculpting. Although I haven't had the time to join a studio up here in Athens, I would love more than anything to start creating pottery again! While still in high school, I competed at the district and state levels in District Project Achievement as a senior 4Her in the Arts & Crafts category, and earned the first place spot at both! In this competition, 4hers across Georgia produced various demonstrations within their category of choice, and I chose to do a demonstration on the various glazing techniques that are presently, as well as most commonly, used in modern pottery and sculpting practices.
If you check out my Creations page, you'll see much more of my art - 3D as well as other mediums!

Favorite Glazing Techniques

  • Brush and Sponge
